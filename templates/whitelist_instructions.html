Why am I seeing reminders in the spam folder?

Email services have strict filters to protect you from unwanted messages. Sometimes legitimate emails, including health reminders, may be incorrectly marked as spam.

By following the instructions below for your email provider, you can ensure our reminders reach your inbox when you need them.

Instructions by Email Provider

Gmail

Method 1: From Spam folder

Open your Spam folder
Find an email from "Medical Reminder"
Select the email (don't open it)
Click "Not Spam" at the top
The email will be moved to your inbox

Method 2: Add to contacts

Open Gmail
Click on the Contacts icon on the right sidebar
Click "Create contact"
Enter "Medical Reminder" as the name
Enter "anshul.saini1507@gmail.com" as the email
Click Save

Method 3: Create a filter

Open Gmail
Click the search options (down arrow) in the search bar
In the "From" field, type "anshul.saini1507@gmail.com"
Click "Create filter"
Check "Never send to Spam"
Click "Create filter"
